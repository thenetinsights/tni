OUR MENUS

Appetizers

Delight your taste buds with our succulent Grilled Shrimp Skewers. Fresh, plump shrimp, marinated in a zesty blend of garlic, lemon, and herbs, grilled to perfection. Served with a tangy citrus aioli dipping sauce, these skewers offer a harmonious balance of smoky flavors and vibrant seasoning. Each bite bursts with a tantalizing medley of grilled goodness, making it an ideal starter to elevate your dining experience.

    Key Ingredients:

  • Fresh jumbo shrimp
  • marinade
  • zest
  • herbs
  • Citrus aioli dipping sauce

Pairing Suggestions:

Complement the exquisite flavors of the Grilled Shrimp Skewers with a crisp white wine or a refreshing citrus-based cocktail. The bright acidity and subtle sweetness of the wine or cocktail perfectly accentuate the grilled shrimp's savory profile.

Allergy Information:

Please inform our staff of any allergies or dietary restrictions. The dish may contain shellfish and traces of gluten in the dipping sauce.

$9.99 - $11.00

Main Course

Indulge in culinary excellence with our Herb-Crusted Filet Mignon. A prime cut of tender beef, delicately coated in a blend of fresh herbs, roasted to perfection, and served with a velvety red wine reduction. Each bite unveils a symphony of flavors – the savory richness of the perfectly seared steak harmonizes with the aromatic herb crust, while the luscious wine reduction adds a luxurious depth to every mouthful. Paired with a medley of seasonal roasted vegetables and creamy mashed potatoes, this dish promises an exquisite dining experience.

Key Ingredients:

  • Prime filet mignon
  • Fresh herb crust (rosemary, thyme, parsley)
  • Red wine reduction
  • Seasonal roasted vegetables
  • Creamy mashed potatoes

    • Pairing Suggestions:

    Elevate your dining experience by pairing the Herb-Crusted Filet Mignon with a robust red wine, such as a Cabernet Sauvignon or Merlot. The wine's bold flavors complement the richness of the steak, enhancing the overall palate sensation.

    Allergy Information:

    Please notify our staff of any allergies or dietary restrictions. The dish may contain dairy in the mashed potatoes and traces of gluten in the herb crust.
    $18.99 - $20.00

    Dessert

    Experience pure indulgence with our Decadent Chocolate Lava Cake. A velvety smooth, molten-centered chocolate cake, baked to perfection and served warm. As you delve into this exquisite creation, the outer layer unveils a rich, moist chocolate cake, giving way to a luscious, gooey center of molten chocolate bliss. Accompanied by a scoop of creamy vanilla bean ice cream and drizzled with a raspberry coulis, each spoonful is a symphony of flavors and textures, a paradise for chocolate enthusiasts.

    Key Ingredients:

  • High-quality dark chocolate
  • Cocoa powder
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla bean ice cream
  • Raspberry coulis

    • Suggestions:

    Complement the indulgent sweetness of the Chocolate Lava Cake with a robust coffee or a dessert wine like a late-harvest Riesling. The coffee's bitterness or the wine's sweetness balances the richness of the chocolate, creating a delightful harmony on the palate.

    Allergy Information:

    Please inform our staff of any allergies or dietary restrictions. The dessert contains dairy, eggs, and traces of gluten.
    $7.99 - $10.99

    Drinks

    We offer a variety of drinks at our restaurant, the following are a few of what's offered:

  • Spiced Sunset Mojito: A tantalizing blend of fresh mint, muddled lime, and a dash of spiced rum, topped with a splash of soda. A perfect harmony of sweet and tangy flavors, invoking the warmth of a tropical sunset.
  • Elderflower Fizz: A delicate concoction of elderflower liqueur, sparkling wine, and a hint of zesty lemon. Effervescent and floral, this effervescent drink is a delightful sip of elegance.
  • Smoky Maple Old Fashioned: Rich bourbon combined with smoky notes of maple syrup, stirred to perfection with a touch of aromatic bitters. Served on the rocks with an orange twist, it’s a sophisticated take on a classic.
  • Tropical Paradise Sangria: A refreshing blend of tropical fruits marinated in white wine and a splash of rum. Bursting with the flavors of pineapple, mango, and a hint of coconut, this sangria is sunshine in a glass.
  • Berry Bliss Mocktail: A non-alcoholic blend of muddled mixed berries, fresh lemon juice, and a splash of soda. Vibrant, tangy, and bursting with fruity flavors, it’s the perfect choice for a refreshing drink without the alcohol.
  • Gingered Mule: A spicy and invigorating mix of ginger beer, vodka, and a squeeze of lime, served over ice with a candied ginger garnish. A modern twist on a classic, this drink has a kick!
  • Caramel Espresso Martini: Indulge in the rich flavors of espresso and velvety caramel, combined with vodka and a touch of coffee liqueur. This sophisticated martini is a dessert in a glass.
