Accomodation

Standard Rooms


Elegant and well-appointed rooms designed for comfort. They usually feature upscale furnishings, high-quality linens, and modern amenities.

Suites

Larger and more luxurious than standard rooms, suites often include separate living and sleeping areas. They may also come with additional amenities such as a kitchenette or a private terrace.

Presidential Suite The most luxurious and spacious suite in the hotel, often reserved for VIP guests or special occasions. It may include multiple bedrooms, a dining area, and exclusive services.

Deluxe Rooms
Similar to standard rooms but with added amenities or more space, deluxe rooms offer a step up in luxury for guests seeking a more premium experience.

Penthouse Suite
Located on the top floor, penthouse suites offer panoramic views and the utmost in luxury. They often include private balconies, spacious living areas, and personalized services.

Boutique Rooms
Unique, individually designed rooms that showcase a distinct theme or style. These rooms often feature custom furnishings and decor.

Family Rooms
Designed to accommodate families, these rooms may have additional beds or connecting rooms to ensure everyone has a comfortable stay.

Accessible Rooms
Specifically designed for guests with mobility challenges, these rooms feature amenities like wider doorways, grab bars, and accessible bathrooms.

Spa Suites
Rooms that include spa-like amenities, such as a jacuzzi or in-room spa services, providing a luxurious and relaxing experience.
