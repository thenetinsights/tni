About Us

Situated in the heart of Maseno, the extensive ground of LN Wings Club contains a fitness centre and spa. Guests can relax in a sun lounger beside the outdoor pool, which is surrounded by palm trees. The elegant and spacious rooms and suites are decorated with warm colours and wooden furniture. They are equipped with a TV, air-conditioning and a private bathroom. Free Wi-Fi is provided. Maseno LN wings Club offers a fine-dining restaurant with a seasonal menu and extensive wine cellar. Guests can enjoy a variety of snack and drinks at the poolside bar, or a cocktail at the Aksum Bar. The 24-hour front desk can organise concierge, Cardillac and baby-sitting services or make an appointment for guests at the on-site hair salon. Free private parking is also available. A shuttle is provided upon request to Kisumu International Airport, only 13 km away. The Railways Golf Course is 15 km away and Impala National Park is within a 9 km drive.

OUR OBJECTIVES

  • Customer Satisfaction: Ensuring that guests have a positive and memorable experience is often the primary objective of any hotel.
  • Occupancy and Revenue: Maximizing occupancy rates and revenue is a key financial objective.
  • Cost Control: Managing operational costs efficiently without compromising service quality is crucial. Reducing wastage, improving energy efficiency, and optimizing staff scheduling are common ways to control costs.
  • Brand Reputation and Loyalty: Building a strong brand and fostering guest loyalty are long-term objectives.
  • Employee Satisfaction: Satisfied and motivated employees are more likely to deliver outstanding service.
  • Quality Assurance: Maintaining high-quality standards is essential. Hotels frequently set objectives related to housekeeping, maintenance, and service quality.
  • Market Share: Hotels aim to capture a significant market share in their respective markets. This may involve increasing advertising and marketing efforts.
  • Sustainability: Many hotels now focus on sustainable and eco-friendly practices as an objective.
  • Health and Safety: Ensuring the safety and security of guests is paramount. Hotels set objectives for maintaining safety standards and having proper emergency response plans in place.
  • Community Engagement: Building strong relationships with the local community can be an objective.
